# 7 Calorie\-Counting Apps ...

**Calorie-counting Apps** can be so very helpful when you're dieting, since they're always available, ready to use on the go, no matter where you are. But some **calorie-counting apps** are better than others, and with so many to choose from, how will you know the good ones from the useless ones? I can help. I've downloaded and tested dozens of **calorie-counting apps**, and these are the ones I like best... here are 7 great **calorie-counting apps**.

## 1. Calorie Counter

_ **Price:** FREE at itunes.apple.com_
Featured in The Today Show, The Wall Street Journal, MSNBC, Marie Claire, and People Magazine, I like this **calorie-counting app** because you can use your voice to enter your food choices, rather than typing them in.

## 2. Calorie Counter & Diet Tracker by MyFitnessPal

![Calorie Counter & Diet Tracker by MyFitnessPal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diet/2012/01/2_calorie-counter-diet-tracker-by-myfitnesspal_320x480.jpg)

**Price:** FREE at [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/calorie-counter-diet-tracker/id341232718)
This **calorie-counting app** has over 1.1 million foods included, which means it's probably got all of the foods you eat every day! The foods are sorted in easy-to-use categories, so everything's easy to find, too.

## 3. Calorie Counter by MyNetDiary

![Calorie Counter by MyNetDiary](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diet/2012/01/3_calorie-counter-by-mynetdiary_334x480.jpg)

**Price:** FREE at [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/calorie-counter-by-mynetdiary/id287529757)
This **calorie-counting app** is perfect for a lazy girl like me - it has a built-in barcode scanner, so you don't have to enter your calories by typing them in. It's also perfect for a cheapie like me, because it's free.

## 4. Calorie Tracker - LIVESTRONG.COM

_ **Price:** $2.99 at itunes.apple.com_
Why would you pay $3 for a **calorie-counting app**, when there are so many free ones on my list? Because this one's the best, and it does a lot more than just keep track of calories. It's more of a directed, goal-oriented food and exercise journal, with one intent: to help you lose weight. It's worth every penny, and still costs less than a fru-fru latte from Starbucks.

## 5. Fast Food Calories

![Fast Food Calories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diet/2012/01/5_fast-food-calories_334x480.jpg)

**Price:** FREE at [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fast-food-calories/id402447013)
Fast food is a fact of modern life, and some of it can actually be diet-friendly. Make good, healthful choices with this calorie counter, pre-loaded with the menus and nutrition information from all of your favorite fast food restaurants.

## 6. Calorie Counter • FREE

_ **Price:** FREE at itunes.apple.com_
It's free. It's easy to use. The interface is intuitive. What else could you ask for in a basic **calorie-counting app**? Nothing. Trust me.

## 7. Calorie Counter by FatSecret

_ **Price:** FREE at itunes.apple.com_
Keep track of the calories you consume and the calories you burn with this handy free **calorie-counting app**. It's easy to sync with your online account, too.

Any one of these **calorie-counting apps** will do the trick, but I especially like number four, by LIVESTRONG. Which of these **calorie-counting apps** do you think you'll like best? Or, if you've used one, which did you prefer, and why? Or is there another **calorie-counting app** you can recommend? Do tell!
