# Yummy Products for Girls Who Are Gluten\-free ...

Being gluten-free means avoiding anything that contains wheat, rye or barley. Doctors recommend this diet for people who have celiac disease or a gluten intolerance. However, some women choose to go [gluten-free](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/naturally-gluten-free-foods-you-dont-have-to-worry-about-enjoying/) for other health reasons. Gluten-free doesn't have to mean without flavor though. With the popularity of gluten-free diets, there are an increasing number of delicious items out there. Try one of these and you'll see just what I mean.
