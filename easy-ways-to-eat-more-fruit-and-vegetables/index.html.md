# Super Simple Ways to Eat More Fruits and Vegetables  ...

Do you need some **easy ways to eat more fruit and vegetables**? Don't we all! This is a common problem. It’s something that we all know we need to do in order to be fit and healthy, but it’s also something that many of us tend to struggle with. It is often seen as sacrilege if you confess to either not liking fruits and vegetables or simply not bothering to eat as many as you know you should, but we’re afraid this is one of those health rules that is very hard to bend. If you want to be as healthy as possible for as long as possible, you need to be eating plenty of fruit and veg! If you know deep down in your heart that you are one of those people who definitely doesn’t consume enough fruit and vegetables, then you might want to read through these **easy ways to eat more fruit and vegetables**.

## 1. Keep It Visible

One of the best **easy ways to eat more fruit and vegetables** is to make them visible. Make the commitment to always have a fully stocked fruit bowl in the main room of your home. Just having it out where you can see it will encourage you to eat more throughout the day. Equally, have a thriving vegetable collection in your kitchen so that you can add the delicious ingredients to pretty much any dish that you might be preparing. Out of sight, out of mind - the exact opposite of that saying is what we are looking for!

## 2. Explore the Produce Aisles

Part of the problem is that many people aren’t even aware of all of the amazing fruit and vegetables that are out there and available to buy! You don’t have to stick to boring old peas, carrots, apples, and oranges. There are hundreds of different kinds of fruits and vegetables that you can sample. Having more variety in your choices makes it much more fun to meet the daily recommended guidelines.

## 3. Skip Potatoes

Sure, potatoes are technically a vegetable, but they are much more slowly digested carbohydrate then pretty much all other types of veg. Try something out: whenever you are planning on adding a potato to your meal, switch it out instead for something like a carrot, turnip, swede, or you could even get on the sweet potato bandwagon because they are much healthier for you overall.

## 4. Veggie Night

If you are a meat eater, then it’s likely that meat, fish or poultry makes up the main ingredient for every meal that you eat during the week. Try to cut down on this by committing to at least one veggie night a week where you make a dinner that is completely meat free. This will encourage you to think of fruit and vegetable alternatives and will help you to see that you can use the products for more than just a snack on their own.

## 5. Smoothies

Smoothies are the absolute best way to trick yourself into consuming more fruit and vegetables! Rather than sitting there and eating a plate full of greens or fruit, you can add them to a mixer and make a delicious and healthy drink from them. What could be better!?
