# Try These 7 Meals That the Hottest Instagrammers Eat for Lunch ...

When you think food porn, you probably think of Instagram. There are multitudes of foodies that document their meals for the rest of us to drool over. Sometimes, they are totally out of reach as you’d have to travel to a different country to find them. In many cases, however, Instagrammers create meals you can copy at home so that lunch is never the same boring bologna sandwich again. Find yourself some accounts to follow, but in the meantime, try these tasty meals.

## 1. Chicken with Steamed Veggies and Fra Diavolo Sauce

Chicken and veggies are great choices for lunchtime because they contain a good mix of fiber and protein, which satisfy your appetite and give you the energy you need to tackle your afternoon. Topping them with a spicy and decadent tasting Fra Diavolo sauce gives you enough flavor to keep you from feeling deprived with your meal. You will not be able to get enough of this meal.

## 2. The Sushirito

If you love sushi for lunch, you are going to adore this meal. It’s all your favorite sushi combined into one handheld pocket. Now you don’t have to decide whether you want the California roll or the tempura shrimp because you can have them both any day of the week. Get ready for envious stares from all your co-workers when you whip this out of your lunchbox.

## 3. Avocado and Tomato Grilled Cheese

Can’t give up your favorite lunchtime sandwich? You don’t have to eat a peanut butter and jelly sandwich every single day though. Whipping up a quick grilled cheese is super fast and easy and adding tomato and avocado is simple and boosts the flavor and appetite suppressing qualities of the sandwich. Have some fruit on the side and you’re all set.

## 4. Eggs on Toast with Sautéed Spinach

Eggs makes a great choice for your noon meal because they are packed with protein, but are low in fat and calories. Top toast with scrambled eggs and serve it with sautéed spinach and you have a meal fit for a queen. Not only does this taste really good, but it’s fast and easy to throw together so you won’t have to worry about running out of time to eat.

## 5. Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

The great thing about cheese for lunch is that it contains protein and small amount of health fats for satiety and energy. Combining mozzarella with a large tomato and a drizzle of balsamic vinegar and olive oil gives you a delicious and hearty lunch. Still hungry? Pair it with a grilled chicken breast or salmon fillet to round things out.

## 6. Antipasto Platter

There’s nothing easier than an antipasto platter for lunch. Simply choose your favorite meats and cheeses and pile them on your chosen crackers. You can pair them with fruit, olives or nuts. What you end up with is a super tasty meal that is packed with nutrition and will give you loads of energy for the long afternoon ahead of you.

## 7. A Bowl of Poke Deliciousness

Poke is a fabulous seafood meal that puts all the best ingredients into one bowl for you to enjoy. The combination of ingredients gives you the best ratio of healthy fats, protein, carbs and vitamins and minerals. You won’t even care about all that once you get a taste of the bowl. I promise you will love it!

Which of these looks like your new favorite lunch? What other Instagram accounts are great for lunch ideas?
