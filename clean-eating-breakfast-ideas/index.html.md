# 5 Clean Eating Breakfast Ideas for Women Who Want a Healthier Diet ...

**Clean eating breakfast ideas** are a great addition to your meal plan. That's because they are easy to prepare, but are also packed with healthy nutrients that support your health. Clean eating is simply choosing foods that are as close to their natural state as possible. That means avoiding most processed foods and sticking with fresh, whole foods. Here are some **clean eating breakfast ideas** to get your day started off just right.

## 1. Avocado Toast

![dish, meal, food, tableware, vegetarian food,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f9/p5/pigt69ki59a70a15e5478466817605_600x600.jpg)

One of the best **clean eating breakfast ideas** is avocado toast. Start by toasting two slices of whole grain bread. Top each with 1/4 of a mashed avocado, tomato slices and a bit of fresh basil. It's so good!

## 2. Banana Nut Oatmeal

![dish, food, porridge, cuisine, steel cut oats,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/vj/i2dz450p59a70a2eb940c584334005_600x750.jpg)

Oats are one of the best things to eat in the morning because they are low in calories and really filling. Combine a 1/2 cup of rolled oats with 1 banana, a cup of unsweetened almond milk (or your favorite milk) and 2 tablespoons of almond butter (or your favorite nut butter). Enjoy!

## 3. Green Smoothie

![green, girl, long hair, grass, drink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c7/ty/o6jwxy4359a70a439433f984564326_600x750.jpg)

You've probably heard all about how great green smoothies are. They are perfect for a clean eating meal plan because you can mix and match ingredients to satisfy your quota of a number of vitamins and minerals. Try blending a handful of fresh kale with a banana, 1/3 of an avocado, 1/2 cup milk and 4 ounces of plain Greek yogurt. Yum!

## 4. Chia Seed Parfait

![dessert, food, parfait, frozen dessert, dish,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sa/gp/x6e5gp2259a70a580c4a1016611224_600x600.jpg)

Chia seeds are a superfood and adding them to your breakfast is a fantastic idea! Start with 3/4 cup of Greek yogurt in your favorite flavor (make sure it's low in sugar though). Top the yogurt with 1 tablespoon of chia seeds and 1/2 cup sliced fruit, such as strawberries, peaches or kiwi.

## 5. Scrambled Eggs with Veggies

![dish, cuisine, food, vegetarian food, italian food,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/db/c95wygni59a70a72a8702213808936_600x600.jpg)

Eggs are perfect for a morning meal because they are loaded with protein and other important nutrients. Scramble 2 eggs with 1 cup spinach and 1/2 cup diced tomatoes in a couple tablespoons of oil. Serve with a piece of fruit, such as an apple or half a grapefruit.

Now that you've had a delicious clean eating breakfast, you're ready to get your day started. What other clean eating breakfast ideas can you add?
