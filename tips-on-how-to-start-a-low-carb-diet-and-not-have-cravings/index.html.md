# 7 Tips on How to Start a Low\-carb Diet and Not Have Cravings ...

Too many carbs is never a good thing, which is why I am always looking for tips on how to start a low-carb diet. It can be really hard to cut most carbs out of your diet and never have cravings for them, but it is possible. If you follow these tips on how to start a low-carb diet, you will be able to tackle your new diet with ease and minimize your cravings for bread, pasta, sweets, and a bunch of other things you know you shouldn’t eat.

## 1. Lettuce is Your New Best Friend

![Lettuce is Your New Best Friend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11115930_6312_500x721.jpg)

Whenever someone goes on a new low-carb diet, the most popular tips on how to start a low-carb diet that I hear is to embrace lettuce in your diet. Not only for the veggie aspect, but because they can replace bread in a lot of your meals. You can still enjoy sandwiches and wraps; just use lettuce instead. You will enjoy all of your delicious favorites without feeling guilty about eating tons of bread.

## 2. Eat More Protein

![Eat More Protein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120024_3447_500x750.jpg)

Protein is a great way to cut cravings and that nagging feeling of hunger all the time Sneak protein into your low-cab diet with turkey, fish, and eggs. You can eat tons of delicious meals like chicken marsala or omelets. They will leave you feeling full, without a carb ever toughing your lips.

## 3. Snacks on Snacks

![Snacks on Snacks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120118_0188_360x450.jpg)

Cravings usually come when we are missing a certain nutrient or mineral in our diet. For example, when you are craving chips, it might mean you are missing some calcium in your diet. Eat some healthier snacking options like sesame seeds or cheese to curb the craving and feel full.. You will also not notice your absence of carbs in your life if you are constantly snacking throughout the day with healthier low-carb choices.

## 4. Enjoy Every Carb

![Enjoy Every Carb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120139_1425_525x791.jpg)

It’s called a low-carb diet, not a no-carb diet. You can still eat the right carbs sparingly. So when you want to kick the cravings for bagels, bread, and pasta, make sure you are enjoying all of the carbs you are allowing yourself. And when you do eat carbs, make sure they are whole grain and not the empty carbs such as those in white bread.

## 5. Dine in Not out

![Dine in Not out](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120520_5175_473x601.jpg)

It is tempting to crave specific foods when you are reading about how delicious they are on a menu. A simple tip to being successful at your low-carb diet is to stay in to eat. You have control over what foods you keep in your kitchen. By surrounding yourself with healthy and low-carb options at home, you won’t have your mind on all of the foods you are missing out on.

## 6. Go Nuts

![Go Nuts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120429_2068_550x825.jpg)

When on a low-carb diet, nuts are another of your new best friends. They are full of fiber, vitamin E, and copper. They are the perfect snack to keep your mind off hunger and cravings and there are tons of delicious varieties of nuts. If you are even thinking about starting a low-carb diet, you might want to make sure you like nuts first, because they will be helping you through your diet like a small nutty support team.

## 7. Run It out

![Run It out](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/11120545_1929_486x700.jpg)

In addition to all of these tips on eating healthy, exercise is a great way to curb cravings. If you are tempted to down some cheesecake or cook some pasta, go on a light jog around your neighborhood. You can clear your mind of cravings and you will see the pounds shed in no time. Most problems you will encounter in your low-carb diet, can be solved with some exercise.

Going low-carb can be hard, but it is not impossible. If you set your mind to it and follow these tips, you will be able to take on your diet with ease.
