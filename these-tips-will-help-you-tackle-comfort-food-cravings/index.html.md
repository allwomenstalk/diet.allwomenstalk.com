# These 7 Tips Will Help You Tackle Comfort Food Cravings ...

As the holidays approach and as the weather is cooler, you may find yourself having more cravings. It is amazing how cool weather can have you craving higher calorie, richer foods. Scientists say that our bodies craves more calories in cooler weather so that we can that extra layer of fat to keep us warm in the winter. But bulk up with more layers of clothing not fat and combat your food cravings. There are many ways to distract yourself from these food cravings and even find a healthier swap along the way.

## 1. Have a Glass of Water

![Have a Glass of Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/z0/cgjmpi48564be469e3ede593278043_500x667.jpg)

Don’t give in that craving for chocolate cake. Have a glass of water to aid your digestion, rid your body of that craving and stay hydrated. Wait 15 minutes and watch your craving subside. And if it does not go away, go for a workout!

## 2. Go for a Workout

![Go for a Workout](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pz/p7/kal707v7564be4b2cc927603663811_500x500.jpg)

Dying for that holiday pecan pie that is sure to comfort your stomach, think again! Go for a workout to get your mind off your food cravings and burn some calories. Get in shape while you curb your food craving! You go girl, you have control over your life.

## 3. Increase Your Protein

![Increase Your Protein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/h9/w42qzwdn564be5284c725558332227_500x500.jpg)

If you find yourself having daily cravings you can be nutritionally deficit. Increase your protein so that you will feel full for longer and have less food cravings. Make a note of the times that you are most hungry and plan to have protein prior to this so you can kick your craving to the curb. You got this!

## 4. Have a Healthier Swap

![Have a Healthier Swap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/cg/yw6999tp564be56463c7b223908172_500x500.jpg)

Craving that apple cobbler on a daily basis in the cool, crisp weather? Create your own healthy swap to save on calories and save your waistline. To prepare this healthy indulgence; dice 2 apples, 1 pear, heat up iron skillet, pour in fruit, 2 teaspoons of cinnamon, 1 Tablespoon of organic pure maple syrup, 2 teaspoon of minced walnuts and ¼ cup of steel cut oats. Simmer on low for 10 minutes. You will create a healthier version of your unhealthy indulgence. There is nothing better than revamping a dish to fulfill a craving without breaking your diet!

## 5. Distract Yourself

![Distract Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/ia/qs70tsmu564be64355c1b353564636_500x500.jpg)

Stop having sugar cookies and pie pulling you off your healthy lifestyle. You can overcome your cravings. Go for a run, walk or hike and think about it. I find every time I do this that by the time I finish the workout, the craving is gone. It is like magic!

## 6. Journal It

![Journal It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/nx/k8u53apn564be73498cb9859655766_600x800.jpg)

Journal your food cravings and the emotions that are involved. There is a good chance that your food cravings are determined by the mood you are in. This is common for most people but the question is, how strong are you? Do not give in. You are worth more than a slice of cake!

## 7. Get to Bed Sleepy Head

![Get to Bed Sleepy Head](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/t8/xh1cnum9564be7ab76ce3172487380_600x476.jpg)

If you are debating on that late night high fat snack, think again! It is late and likely you are very tired. You can easily be confusing boredom and being tired with your food cravings. So sleep on it and you can revisit your craving in the morning with a nutritious omelet!

So go ahead swap out those unhealthy cravings, distract yourself, have some water; have control of your life. You only have only life so it is time for you to live the life you deserve! Are you ready to combat your food cravings?
