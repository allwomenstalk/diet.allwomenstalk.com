# The Best Foods for Your Zodiac Sign ...

What are the **the best foods for your zodiac sign**? When it comes to lifestyle and zodiac signs, you will have seen countless articles recommending different workouts, different romantic advice and everything in between based on your individual star sign. If you happen to hold a lot of faith in astrology, then there is information to be gleaned related to pretty much anything and everything in your life, and food is certainly no exception. Here is a list of some of the **the best foods for your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aquarius

You prefer a no-fuss attitude to food, things that don’t need too much altering to cook. Chestnuts, tofu, wild rice, parsnips and Brussel sprouts are all great options.

## 2. Pisces

When it comes to your food preferences, a Pisces always leans toward delicate flavours that are simply prepared. Lentils, eggplant, sweet potato, honey and pears, these are all perfect foods for you that fit the bill.

## 3. Aries

You love anything that can be eaten quickly to match your fast-paced lifestyle, but that still has nutritional value without much preparation needed, so things like olives, eggs, couscous and asparagus are all perfect.

## 4. Taurus

You like to eat slowly and to savour your food, so creamy dishes like quiche and chocolate mousse are great. For healthier options, go for coconut milk and avocados, still creamy and luxurious!

## 5. Gemini

You like a lot of mental stimulation, and this can be found in interesting tastes like tangerines, feta cheese, dill and even snap peas. You also would benefit from adding cucumber to your water.

## 6. Cancer

Cancers tend to be sensitive, and so do their stomachs! You prefer some good old fashioned comfort food over the fancy stuff, but things like coconut, pine nuts, and even lobster shouldn’t be overlooked!

## 7. Leo

You like to make flamboyant dishes to match your personality, so tomatoes, watermelon, mustard and mozzarella are all things that you should have in your cupboards on a regular basis.

## 8. Virgo

You like to eat in small, nourishing bursts rather than big heavy meals, so foods like figs, raspberries, almonds and shrimp are all perfect for your personality and needs.

## 9. Libra

For you, eating is an art form, one to be enjoyed and experimented with. Something to be aware of is staying on top of portion size, so small mouthfuls like walnuts, scallops, fennel, capers, apples and pomegranates help to satisfy this.

## 10. Scorpio

You are all about the intensity of a good food, always seeking things with big, bold flavours. A few to always have around include pumpkin, mushrooms, chocolate, beets and even oysters.

## 11. Sagittarius

Sagittarians really buy into the ‘you are what you eat’ way of thinking, so it’s always best to keep things nice and healthy with turkey, plums, pecans, squash and broccoli.

## 12. Capricorn

You like to keep things traditional and reminiscent of your childhood, which paves the way for foods like beef, cauliflower, cranberries, leeks and kale.
